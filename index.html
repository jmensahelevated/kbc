About the Camping

Welcome to the first-ever Greater Accra Regional Youth Camping event, Kingdom Builders Camp 2024. This camp is dedicated to empowering young Christians to become strong, impactful leaders in their communities.

At the Anagkazo Bible Ministry and Training Center, you will embark on a transformative journey that blends education, worship, and fellowship. Our mission is to nurture and equip the next generation of Kingdom Builders with the knowledge, faith, and skills they need to make a lasting impact.

BENEFITS

Kingdom Builders Camp offers spiritual growth, church building, and personal empowerment, making it a transformative experience.

Spiritual Growth and Renewal

In the serene environment of a camp, free from the hustle and bustle, participants can focus on their spiritual journey. The structured sessions of KBC 24 in it's worship and teachings help believers to reassess their faith, seek God earnestly, and receive His guidance. This renewal of spirit empowers them to face lifeâ€™s challenges with a renewed vigor and trust in God.

Building Lasting Christian Relationships

The communal aspect of camp meetings is invaluable. Through group activities, shared meals, and collective worship, participants forge deep connections. These relationships often extend beyond the camp, providing a network of support that helps maintain and grow their faith. Encouraging each other and holding one another in their spiritual walk.

Hebrews 10:24-25

Empowerment for Service and Leadership

With KBC 2024, individuals receive targeted training and inspiration to utilize their God-given gifts for the benefit of others. Whether through teaching, organizing, or leading, they return to their church with a renewed sense of purpose and the skills necessary to serve effectively. This empowerment is crucial for our generation leading them to Christ.

1 Peter 4:10
